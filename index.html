Roadmap

Our vision extends beyond traditional boundaries. House of Hamsters
offers a unique blend of thrilling poker gameplay and the potential to earn
cryptocurrency rewards.

1st and 2nd Quarter

Market research on NFT games and poker.

Game concept formation, defining key features and mechanics.

Tokenomics and game economic model creation.

Mini-game development to attract audience and test gameplay mechanics.

Initiation of RNG model development with open-source code to ensure fairness of gameplay.

Launching mini-game for gathering feedback and assessing audience interest.

Integration with Solana blockchain for NFT asset creation and management.

Development and testing of smart contracts for secure and transparent NFT trading.

Blockchain wallet development for storing and managing NFT assets.

Optimization of on-chain transactions to enhance network speed and efficiency.

Genesis NFT collection sales round to attract investors and collectors.

Minimum Viable Product (MVP) poker game development with basic features and NFT asset management.

Main game testing among a limited audience to identify bugs and flaws.

3rd and 4th Quarter

Feedback analysis and user interface and gameplay optimization based on testing results.

Addition of extra poker game variations and modes to increase player diversity and engagement.

Smart contract audit to ensure their security and reliability.

Integration with Biswap DEX to enhance accessibility and liquidity of game tokens.

Launch of internal NFT marketplace for trading and exchange of unique assets.

Commencement of mobile application development to expand audience reach and usability.

Commencement of NFT table sales for poker, providing unique gaming opportunities and advantages.
